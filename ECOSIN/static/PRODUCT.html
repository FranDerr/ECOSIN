IMMERSE YOURSELF IN THE ECOSIN WORLD

Four actions in one, just by inserting a bag.

INSERTION

Located above the product, you will find a hole for inserting the waste.

READING

In this phase, a reader, through a sensor, will recognize the waste material and channel it towards the suitable container.

RESIZING

At this point the waste will be shredded to the point of taking up little space.

BOXING

In the final stage, the resized waste will be placed in a box and ready for collection.
